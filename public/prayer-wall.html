The Prayer Wall

A House of Prayer for Everyone

God has wonderful plans for each one of us. But how do we access those plans?

One way is through prayer. In the Bible, God promises us that if we pray, He will hear us; if we look for him, we will find Him; and if we need help, He will rescue us. But how do we pray?

Prayer is not complicated. Prayer is simply a conversation with God. A conversation which He has already started through His word. It's a conversation to which everyone is invited. When we pray together it's just a bigger conversation.

We believe in the power of corporate prayer. God has always wanted a 'house of prayer' a praying community - where everyone is welcome that's what we want to be.

So, why don't you come and join us and pray on our online PRAYER WALL.
