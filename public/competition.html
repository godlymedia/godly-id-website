A Global Initiative for Kingdom Impact

Our Roots

Since 2015, SUG & partners have been hosting community competitions, entrepreneurship training, workshops and motivational events across communities in Africa, UK & USA.

The Collaboration

Leveraging these experiences, Godly.id have teamed up with SUG & Its Partners to host the 1st round of the "+++++ competition" dedicated to encouraging entrepreneurs, socioprenures, missionaries, evangelist, street pastors and community loving purpose driven individuals to birth the piloting of creative scalable bite sized initiatives which share Gods love and bring lasting transformation by either winning souls, birthing transformation or solving a problem in their community.

Our Heart

Our dedication is to support the exploration of how through an idea to "Show Love" to the community, participants can pilot project or ventures to reality.

The Initiative

This initiative is designed to steer up and promote the concepts of creative evangelism and the sharing of Gods love in ways that bring lasting transformation to communities in need across the nations by empowering purpose-driven individuals to launch scalable bite sized projects and ventures that demonstrate God's love, win souls, and bring lasting change to communities in need. We exist to help you ignite your creative juices and turn a simple idea into a real, impactful pilot project.

Global Invitation

We are particularly keen to receive applications in all categories from participants in the Africa, Asia and South America. And warmly welcome creative evangelism application from Europe and North America.

Our Core Objectives

Promote Practical Evangelism

Demonstrating God's love through tangible solutions that bring transformation.

Empower Entrepreneurs

Support innovators building projects aligned with Kingdom values.

Increase Education

Equip participants with knowledge to start and grow business projects and Evangelism.

Grow Digital Engagement

Share inspiring stories of love, faith, and impact across digital platforms.
