A Global Initiative for Kingdom Impact

Since 2015, SUG and its partners have empowered communities through entrepreneurship and evangelism. Now, in partnership with Godly.id, we present the Love Evangelism & Transformation Competition. This initiative is designed to empower purpose-driven individuals to launch scalable projects that demonstrate God's love, win souls, and bring lasting change to communities in need. We exist to help you turn a simple idea into a real, impactful pilot project.

Our Core Objectives

Promote Practical Evangelism

Go beyond words by demonstrating God's love through tangible solutions that bring spiritual and social transformation.

Empower Entrepreneurs

Support innovators building projects aligned with Kingdom values.

Increase Education

Equip participants with practical knowledge to strengthen evangelism.

Grow Digital Engagement

Share inspiring stories of love, faith, and impact across digital platforms to inspire a global audience.
